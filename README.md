This project analyzes customer income levels, purchasing potential, and geographic distribution to determine the most suitable product to advertise to each customer. The goal of the analysis is to help an online clothing retailer design a targeted marketing campaign that recommends the right product to the right customer.
Using Power Query for data preparation and Power BI for visualization, the dashboard explores income distribution, customer segmentation, purchasing potential, and behavioral patterns such as product returns and customer ratings. These insights help businesses identify which customers are more likely to purchase low-cost, mid-range, or premium products. By segmenting customers based on income levels and predicted purchasing power, the company can improve marketing efficiency, increase conversion rates, and maximize revenue from personalized product recommendations.
